Q: How can I contact the Daily Bulletin?

You can contact us in a number of ways. The simplest way to reach us is through the comment form provided on this site. We can be reached during business hours weekdays.

If you are looking for a story that previously appeared on this Website, please call +604-9995 7600 or email to editor@dailybulletin.com

Q: Whom do I contact and what information do you need me to send when I discover that there is a problem with your Web site?

If you experience a problem navigation or any problem on our sites, you can directly message on the feedback form and describe in as much detail as possible, including the stpes you take leading up to it, how often it occurs, and the exact text of any error message that you receive. The information you provide will help us to find a solution more quickly.
